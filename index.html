Telehealth Utilization: Medicaid and CHIP
Data Exploration

This dashboard reflects national telehealth utilization data for Medicaid and the Children's Health Insurance Plan (CHIP) beneficiaries between 2018 and 2020. The telehealth services covered include live audio/video, remote patient monitoring, store and forward, and other telehealth methods that fall outside of the previous categories. These metrics are based on data in the T-MSIS Analytic Files (TAF).
